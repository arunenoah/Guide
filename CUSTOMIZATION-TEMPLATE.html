10. Customization Checklist

Before publishing your customized policy, complete this checklist:

  • All company information fields filled in
  • Company-specific restrictions documented
  • Compliance requirements updated
  • Role-based access levels customized
  • Violation consequences defined
  • Technology stack updated
  • Internal standards links provided
  • Contact information updated
  • Deployment procedures customized
  • Audit procedures defined
  • Legal review completed (required!)
  • Security team approval obtained
  • Management sign-off received
  • Policy distributed to all developers
  • Training scheduled for team
  • Acknowledgment process set up (HRIS or digital)

11. Implementation Timeline
